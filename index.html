Varvara romaniuk

My name is Varvara Romaniuk, and I am a young Ukrainian artist. My creative journey began in my childhood. At the age of 6, I enrolled in an evening school of figurative art to acquire basic skills in painting and sculpture. At the age of 12, I graduated from that school, and art has never left me since. My art is an ode to femininity and emotions.

After moving to England, I enrolled in university and decided to pursue a direction in 3D and 2D animation, with the goal of creating videos and cartoons in the future
